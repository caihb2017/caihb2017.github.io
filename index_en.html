Welcome to the Digital Media Technology Team

The Digital Media Technology Team is led by Professor Cai Hongbin of the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China--School of Computer Science and Engineering. The team has long been engaged in research on 3D graphics algorithms, image and video processing, 3D graphics engines, and network applications, with strong project organization and R&D capabilities. The team includes over 20 professors, associate professors, and graduate students.

In terms of scientific research, the team has led or participated in 1 National Natural Science Foundation project, 6 National "863" Program projects (including 1 key project and 1 goal-oriented project), 1 National Support Program project, 2 MIIT-funded projects, 2 bidding projects, 1 Doctoral Fund project, 5 Sichuan Provincial Science and Technology Projects, 1 Sichuan Provincial Youth Science and Technology Fund project, and 1 Chengdu Science and Technology Project. The team has applied for 14 PCT patents and been granted 26 Chinese invention patents. They have won 1 National Science and Technology Progress Award (Second Prize), 1 Sichuan Provincial Science and Technology Progress Award (First Prize), and 2 Second Prizes.
